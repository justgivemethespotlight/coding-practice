BMW M5 Competition F90 2023

The 2023 BMW M5 Competition (F90) represents the pinnacle of pure internal combustion M-engineering, blending executive luxury with supercar-slaying performance. At its heart lies the sophisticated 4.4-liter V8 M TwinPower Turbo engine (S63), meticulously tuned to deliver a staggering 617 horsepower (625 PS) and 553 lb-ft (750 Nm) of torque. This immense power is managed by a lightning-fast 8-speed M Steptronic transmission, propelling the sedan from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.3 seconds. With the optional M Driver’s Package, the top speed is derestricted to an exhilarating 305 km/h (190 mph). F90 apart is the highly advanced M xDrive system. This rear-biased all-wheel-drive system ensures maximum traction in all conditions, but for those seeking a raw, analog experience, it features a dedicated 2WD mode. This allows the driver to send 100% of the power to the rear wheels, enabling effortless drifts and classic BMW handling characteristics. The Competition variant further enhances dynamics with a 7mm lower ride height, increased camber, and stiffer engine mounts, providing laser-sharp steering precision and mid-corner stability.
